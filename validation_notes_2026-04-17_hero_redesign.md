# Lemonade-inspired hero validation

The Lemonade homepage reference was reviewed directly before implementation. The hero effect appears to rely on a clean centered editorial composition with layered illustration and depth cues, rather than a literal embedded 3D video or canvas scene.

The Cardone hero was redesigned to adapt that structure into the existing black-and-gold brand language. The updated live preview now uses a centered headline zone with staged faux-3D side panels, a floating monogram marker, and stronger depth framing around the copy.

The redesign preserves unobstructed primary and secondary calls to action, keeps the bilingual navigation intact, and continues to compile successfully in production. The only build note remains the existing non-blocking bundle-size warning.
